BIM Dimension

Here is below, a brief description of the current seven dimensions of BIM:

  1. 2nd dimension is considered the documentation,The two geometric dimensions XY.
  2. 3rd dimension is considered the space,The three geometric dimensions XYZ Without them the BIM would not be, They allow visualization and representation.
  3. 4th dimension is considered the time,Adds a "time" data to the three geometric dimensions it allows visualize in time the duration of an event or the progress of a construction phase.
  4. 5th dimension is considered a cost,Adds the "cost" data to the 4 previous dimensions it Allows to estimate the construction costs or get an overview of the financial situation of a project at a given moment.
  5. 6th dimension is helps perform energy consumption analyses, It also allows for measurement and verification during building occupation, and improved processes for gathering lessons learned in high performance facilities.
  6. 7th dimension the 7D model as constructed contains all the information necessary to the owner for the use and maintenance of the building.
